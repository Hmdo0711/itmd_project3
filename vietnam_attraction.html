Famous Vietnam Tourist Attraction

Ha Long Bay

Ha Long Bay has been recognized by Unesco many times as a World Natural Heritage Site with thousands of islands created by a magnificent and lively creation. Ha Long Bay has beautiful scenery, so it is a very attractive tourist destination for domestic and international tourists.

Hue City

The ancient capital of Hue consists of 3 citadel: Imperial Citadel, Imperial Citadel and Forbidden City. With the ancient, magnificent scenery and unique architectural works, you will be immersed in the old royal life and learn more about the historical period of the Nguyen Dynasty.

Da Nang

Da Nang is a coastal city with a beach more than 60 km long. With a beautiful beach, gentle stretching and endless white sand, My Khe beach Da Nang was voted by Forbes magazine of the US as one of the six most attractive beaches on the planet. Diving in the clear water, playing with the waves and sunbathing on the fine white sand is enough to give anyone a feeling of relaxation after hours of work. Not only that, there are many sea services for you to experience such as canoeing, parasailing, windsurfing, banana boating, water motor, scuba diving to see corals.

Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park

Phong Nha - Ke Bang National Park is a world heritage recognized by UNESCO in 2003, not only preserving intact geology and geomorphology, but also hiding many mysteries of nature, which are majestic caves and caves. in the heart of limestone mountains that were formed millions of years ago.

Phu Quoc Island

Phu Quoc is a beautiful archipelago located deep in the Gulf of Thailand, in Kien Giang province. In the southern sea of ​​the country, Ngoc Phu Quoc island - the largest island of Vietnam, is also the largest island in a population of 22 islands here. Clear sea water, peaceful streams and many unique seafood are the great advantages of Phu Quoc tourism.
